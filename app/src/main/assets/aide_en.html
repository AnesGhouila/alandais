Oburo.O

This application makes working hours counting easy as a simple touch of the screen. The calculation is automatic. The alternation between check in and out work is automatic.

Features

Ckeck-in/out

To add a check-in/out from the application, just use the "Fast check-in/out" button. The time of the check-in/out will be set from the phone clock. First hit on the button is the check-in, the second will be the check-out. If you need, you can use the "Insert a check-in/out" button to add a time in the past or future.

Add comments

To add a comment on a check-in, just click on the check-in in the calendar view. Use cancel and Ok buttons.

Export

It is possible to save your check-in in CSV format (Excel) by email. Your email address must be set in the parameters menu. You can alse change the format of the CSV file in this screen.

List your check-in/out

In the view Calendar, you can list all your check-in/out, by day, week, month or year. Just select a date in the date picker, and then the period you want to see. The list is updated real-time.

Widget

A widget is available, to fast check or to see the current state of your working time.

Parameters

Email address

This is the e-mail adress that will be used in order to receive saving data. E-mail sending will be launched since an Android e-mail application that will be choosen by the user at saving time.

Separator

The separator will be the one selected during saving in CSV format. This allows to collect directly check in in an Excel table.
